MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
(Synthetic sample for demonstration — not a real contract)

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Harbor Labs LLC and
Meridian Devices GmbH (each a "Party").

1. PURPOSE. The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship and may
disclose confidential information to each other.

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION. "Confidential Information" means any non-public business
or technical information disclosed by one Party to the other.

3. OBLIGATIONS.
3.1 The Receiving Party shall protect Confidential Information using the same degree of
care it uses for its own confidential information.
3.2 The confidentiality obligations in this Agreement shall survive indefinitely and
shall continue in perpetuity after termination.

4. OWNERSHIP OF DEVELOPMENTS. Any inventions, ideas or improvements conceived by either
Party during the discussions shall become the sole and exclusive property of Harbor
Labs LLC.

5. NON-SOLICITATION. For a period of three (3) years, neither Party shall solicit or
hire any employee of the other Party.

6. INDEMNIFICATION. Each Party shall indemnify the other against any and all losses
arising from any disclosure, without limitation of liability.

7. TERM. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for five (5) years.

8. RETURN OF MATERIALS. Upon request, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy
Confidential Information.

9. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Germany.
